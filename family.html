Statistics suggest that 42% of marriages in England and Wales end in divorce.


Assisting our clients through the distress of divorce is of the upmost importance at Hattin Solicitors.
We look at the needs of the whole family, particularly the children, minimizing the stress a divorce creates. Hattin Solicitors may assist you in a range of issues including the following areas:

  • Divorce/separation

  • Living together

  • Child maintenance

  • Domestic abuse

  • Financial settlements

  • Pre-nuptial agreements

  • Collaborative agreements

Common law is the basis of the legal system of England and Wales. There is no codified system of law. Family law is found in acts of parliament and applied and interpreted by the higher courts to create legal precedent. At Hattin Solicitors with our legal expertise we will use this as a basis to help find out what your rights are and help resolve your matter.

At a testing time, concise and friendly legal advise is what is necessary.

At Hattin Solicitors it is imperative to us that our clients feel comfortable and know that we understand their situation. Once we are in contact and have that understanding we can advice the appropriate path for you to take which could be collaborative law, mediation or a traditional court based solution.
